Omeprazole

Medicine Details

  • Drug Interactions: Clopidogrel, methotrexate, warfarin, HIV medications, antifungals.
  • Drug-Food Interactions: Avoid alcohol and highly acidic foods (e.g., citrus juices).
  • Adverse Effects: Headache, nausea, diarrhea, stomach pain, flatulence, dizziness.
  • Contraindications: Hypersensitivity, severe liver disease.
  • Precautions: Long-term use may increase the risk of bone fractures.
