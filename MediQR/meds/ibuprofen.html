Ibuprofen

Medicine Details

  • Drug Interactions: Aspirin, warfarin, oral hypoglycemics, methotrexate, BP medications, clopidogrel, lithium, cyclosporine.
  • Drug-Food Interactions: Alcohol, cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cabbage. Take with food to reduce irritation.
  • Adverse Effects: Nausea, indigestion, constipation, diarrhea, dizziness, stomach pain, bleeding, ulceration, rash, fluid retention.
  • Contraindications: Hypersensitivity, gastrointestinal bleeding, peptic ulceration, serious heart conditions, liver & kidney failure, pregnancy.
  • Precautions: Check with your doctor immediately if any adverse effects occur.
  • Missed Dose: If you miss a dose, take it as soon as possible. If it is almost time for the next dose, skip the missed dose. Do not double doses.
  • Dosage:
    • For menstrual cramps: Adults - 400 mg every 6 hours.
    • For headache and stomach ache: Adults - 400 mg every 6 hours. Infants - Dose must be determined by a doctor based on body weight.
