Telmisartan

Medicine Details

  • Drug Interactions: ACE inhibitors, NSAIDs, potassium supplements, lithium.
  • Drug-Food Interactions: Avoid potassium-rich foods (e.g., bananas) and alcohol.
  • Adverse Effects: Dizziness, headache, fatigue, upper respiratory infection, hypotension.
  • Contraindications: Pregnancy, severe kidney or liver impairment.
  • Precautions: Monitor potassium levels during use.
  • Missed Dose: Take as soon as remembered. Skip if close to the next dose.
  • Dosage:
    • For hypertension: Adults - 40-80 mg once daily.
