Amoxicillin

Medicine Details

  • Drug Interactions: Methotrexate, allopurinol, anticoagulants, oral contraceptives.
  • Drug-Food Interactions: Minimal interactions; can be taken with or without food.
  • Adverse Effects: Nausea, diarrhea, rash, headache, fungal infections, allergic reactions.
  • Contraindications: Hypersensitivity to penicillins or cephalosporins.
  • Precautions: Report severe diarrhea or allergic reactions immediately.
  • Missed Dose: Take as soon as remembered, but skip if itâ€™s near the next dose.
  • Dosage:
    • For adults: 500 mg every 8 hours or 875 mg every 12 hours.
    • For children: Dose based on body weight as prescribed by a doctor.
